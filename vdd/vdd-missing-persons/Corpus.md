# Nouns/Objects
-  #Disappeared: individuals who no longer have communication with their historical network
-  #Loved_ones: the network, or individual, persons affected by the loss AND seeking resolution and potentially reconnection
- #Mental_Health: the mental wellness and capacity of a person

# Verbs/Actions

- #closure #bringing_closure: finding accurate information that provides a resolution to the ambiguity, a sense of peace after the loss, and making of meaning
- #anchors : concrete realities the loved ones can hold onto when everything else is up in the air.
- #dissappear_info: open, voluntary and factual data about the disappeared.
- #ambiguous_grief: a type of mourning that occurs when there is a lack of closure from a partner abandoning their family without explanation, forced separations, or long military deployments. The key difference between grief and ambiguous grief is the element of the unknown – the persistent thought that you have no idea what has happened or what the future holds.
- #communication: voluntary exchanges of communication between #Loved_ones and the #Disappeared in the effort to bring #stability and #bringing_closure 
- #educate: providing information that alleviates the uncertainty or mystery of a process, the state of the process, or policy.
- #stability: resolving the situation to the point it does not continue to decline
