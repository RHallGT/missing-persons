# Nouns/Objects
- **disappeared:** individuals who no longer have communication with their historical network
- **loved ones:** the network, or individual, persons affected by the loss AND seeking resolution and potentially reconnection

# Verbs/Actions
- **"Bringing Closure":** finding accurate information that provides a resolution to the ambiguity, a sense of peace after the loss, and making of meaning
- **Communication:** open, voluntary and factual data about the disappeared.
