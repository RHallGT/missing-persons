#external_resolution Finite States:
- #Loved_Ones and #Disappeared want to connect
- #Loved_Ones want to connect, but #Disappeared do not
- #Loved_Ones do not want to connect, but the #Disappeared do
- #Loved_Ones or #Disappeared want to send read only / no reply messages

#external_resolution States, in general, do not change a lot

#internal_resolution have to do more with the #Loved_Ones resolution than with reconnecting with the #Disappeared
