# Vision:
"Bringing Closure" to all persons affected by missing persons.

# Intentions:
- Helping those persons affected achieve
	- internal resolution
		- is the person-affected mature enough to understand and accept closure?
		- is the reality of the situation acceptable?
		- is it about reclaiming "wholeness", apart from what was lost?
		- what meaning is necessary to extract from the situation?
	- external resolution
		- has communication broken down due to
			- anticipatable physical factors (lost phone, changed numbers, etc)
		- mental health of one or both persons-affected or missing person
		- physically detained, incarcerated, or otherwise removed by law
- Protecting the privacy and security of those who are escaping abuse or persecution.
